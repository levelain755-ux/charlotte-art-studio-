Meet Charlotte 遇见导师 | Meet Charlotte

Turning professional storytelling
into creative enlightenment. 将专业叙事
化为创造力的启蒙。

“As a comic creator deeply rooted in the top-tier international publishing industry, I know that 'visual language' holds the power to transform and heal.

Founding Charlotte Art Studio is not just about teaching techniques; it's about translating 'professional visual storytelling' into creative enlightenment tailored for youth. We believe every child is born an artist—all they need is the right light, materials filled with possibilities, and an industry guide who truly understands them.”

“作为一名在国际顶级出版界深耕多年的漫画创作者，我深知‘视觉语言’拥有改变和治愈的力量。

创办 Charlotte Art Studio，不仅是为了传授绘画与手工的技巧，更是致力于将‘专业级别的视觉叙事’转化为属于青少年的创造力启蒙。我们相信，每一个孩子都是天生的艺术家，他们需要的只是一束对的光线、充满可能性的材料，以及一位懂他们的行业向导。”

Creative Programs 核心艺术课程 | Creative Programs

A laboratory to ignite imagination. 激发想象的实验场。

Kids & Teens Kids & Teens

Art Foundations 美术基础
(Art Foundations)

Develop a systematic understanding of visual arts. Through focused study of proportions, shading, perspective, and color harmony, build essential techniques that apply to any medium. 系统建立视觉艺术认知。通过对比例、透视、光影与色彩法则的深度训练，夯实关键基本功。这份稳固的基石能够无缝衔接未来任何媒介的高阶艺术创作。

Kids (5-10 y/o) Kids (5-10岁)

Illustration & Aesthetics 插画与审美
(Illustration & Aesthetics)

Perfect for budding young artists. Explore color theory, line work, and imaginative composition using various drawing materials to build a strong artistic and aesthetic foundation. 为儿童打造的综合艺术启蒙课。在绘画实践中探索色彩感知、线条表现与创意构图，培养扎实的造型能力与不可多得的高级审美视角。

Teens (10-18 y/o) Teens (10-18岁)

Comic Creation 漫画创作
(Comic Creation)

Say goodbye to mechanical tracing. Learn professional visual storytelling, from script formulation and character design to advanced storyboarding, guided by a published author. 告别机械临摹，迈向专业级视觉叙事。跟随资深出版漫画家，系统学习剧本构思、原创新角色设定与进阶分镜技巧，将想象化为连载漫画作品。

All Ages All Ages (全年龄段)

Handmade Clay 手工粘土
(Handmade Clay)

Bring whimsical ideas to life in 3D. Experience the tactile joy of molding clay to cultivate exceptional spatial reasoning and a deeper understanding of volume and form. 将平面奇思妙想转化为3D立体世界。在充满手作温度的课堂中，通过感知粘土极其丰富的材质肌理，培养极佳的空间构造思维与扎实的立体造型能力。

Teens (10-18 y/o) Teens (10-18岁)

Digital Art 数码绘画
(Digital Art)

Master industry-standard tools. Starting with Procreate basics, learn brush application, layer logic, and digital effects to bring your artwork to life on screen. 掌握最前沿的数字创作工具。从熟练操作 Procreate 软件入门，系统学习各类笔刷运用、图层逻辑与电绘特效，让原本的画技在屏幕上焕发专业级新生。
